Frequently Asked Questions

Are your treats safe for pets with allergies?

Yes! Our treats are crafted with simple, natural ingredients and minimal preservatives. If your pet has specific allergies or dietary needs, contact us directly for personalized guidance.

Where are your treats made?

All treats are baked in small batches in my own home, with care and quality in mind. I'm located in Walnut, Iowa.

How long will my order take to arrive?

Once baked, treats are packaged and shipped within 1-3 business days. Depending on your location, most orders arrive within 3-7 business days, for an average of about two weeks from purchase to your door. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

At this time, we only ship within the United States. This ensures that every treat arrives fresh, safe, and ready to be enjoyed by your pets.

Do you offer custom flavors or special orders?

If there’s a particular ingredient or flavor your pets love that isn’t currently available, reach out to us. We may be able to accommodate special requests depending on ingredient availability and timing.

What's the best way to contact you?

Since you're already on this page, I recommend using the contact form above. It'll help create an email and it's the most efficient way to get in touch! Alternatively, you can contact me directly via email, Facebook, Instagram, or X.

Can humans eat the treats?

While treats are designed for pets, they are made with safe, natural ingredients. They aren’t sweetened, so they’ll taste bland to us humans, but are safe to sample if you’re curious!
