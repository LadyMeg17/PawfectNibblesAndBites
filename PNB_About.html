Our Origin Story

The idea of Pawfect Nibbles & Bites began in May 2025, shortly after we adopted Thor. His allergies meant we had to rethink what went into his food and treats. I (Meghan) started experimenting with baking homemade treats using simple, natural ingredients that were safe for him to eat.


What started as careful experimentation quickly became a passion that blended my love of baking with my love for my pets. With a culinary background and a science driven, analytical mind, I've spent hours researching and perfecting recipes that are not only allergy-friendly but also delicious (according to the pets) and nutritious. Each treat is crafted with care, ensuring that every bite is packed with flavor and free from common allergens.


While not necessarily recommended for human consumption, they are completely safe for humans to eat as well. We will not feed our pets anything that we wouldn't eat ourselves, and most batches have been sampled before and after cooking. Curiosity got the better of us more than once! There are no added sugars in the treats, so to humans they taste like very bland cookies.

What Makes Our Treats Different

Pawfect Nibbles & Bites treats are made with simple, natural ingredients and minimal preservatives. Because freshness matters, our treats have shorter shelf lives by design. All treats baked in small batches within a week of an order being placed so your pets get treats that haven’t been sitting around for months. If you're looking for a specific ingredient that's not currently available, or if your pets have allergies to any of our current treats, please reach out to us directly and we can look into additional options.

How Orders Work

Each order is prepared in small batches to keep treats fresh and thoughtfully made. Most treats are baked to order or within a week of purchase. Once baked, treats are carefully packaged and shipped out within 1-3 business days. Depending on your location, you can expect to receive your order within 3-7 business days. This leaves the average time from purchase to your door of two weeks. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for an additional fee. If you need a rush order, please contact us directly prior to purchase and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.


Shipping: for now, we’re only shipping within the United States. That way, we can make sure every treat arrives fresh, safe, and ready to be loved. We wish we could send them worldwide, but international shipping gets tricky (and expensive!).

Why We Do What We Do

At Pawfect Nibbles & Bites, we believe that pets deserve treats made with the same care and quality as those we enjoy ourselves. Our treats are the only pet treat line built around allergy awareness and simplicity. Each batch is thoughtfully baked, minimally preserved, and free from unnecessary extras.
